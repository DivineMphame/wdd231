NextRide Car Rental

Fleet of Cars to Explore

NextRide aims to provide a user-friendly platform for customers to rent cars online, offering a wide range of vehicles, competitive pricing, and excellent customer service.

Site Purpose

The site will provide information on car rental and the benefits of travelling in luxurious cars at low cost.

Scenarios

  • A user wants to compare different car models and their features before making a rental decision.
  • A customer wants to rent a car for a weekend trip and needs to know the available options and prices.

Color Schema

Primary Color: #0044cc (Headings, Navigation, Footer)

Secondary Color: #f4f4f4 (Background)

Typography

Main Font: Arial, sans-serif (Body, Paragraphs)

Heading Font: Arial, sans-serif (Headings)
